    Our Story:

    Sustainable City - Philadelphia began as a simple desire shared by three MIS Temple students who wanted to help solve a trash problem they encountered while attending Temple University. After combining their MIS skills, they assisted in the development of this website to aid the temple community in creating a cleaner and more environmentally friendly campus. By guiding students to the appropriate resources, assisting in the reporting of waste problems, and creating a platform for exchanging usable items instead of purchasing new ones.
